We make efforts to reduce the miseries faced by the children in the capital. Education is the basis of development; thus, we provide education to the girls to enhance their and the country's future. Our Mission

Dream Girl Foundation was established with a deep commitment to empower underprivileged girls and provide them with the tools they need to build a better future. We believe every girl deserves access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities to reach her full potential.

Through our Non-Formal Education centers, healthcare initiatives, and empowerment programs, we work to reduce the hardships faced by children in underserved communities. We focus on holistic development - addressing educational needs, health concerns, and providing skill development opportunities.

Since 2003, we have successfully organized multiple campaigns, established NFE centers across communities, and provided medical care to dozens of children. Every girl we serve is reminded that she has the power to create positive change in her life and her community.
